RoCo-Spring: The Robust Correspondence Challenge

A challenge on robust dense correspondence under realistic distribution shifts, covering optical flow, stereo matching, and scene flow.

More information coming soon.

Overview

RoCo-Spring targets the gap between clean benchmark accuracy and robustness under realistic corruptions such as camera noise, adverse weather, blur, compression, and illumination changes.

Tracks

  • Optical flow
  • Stereo matching
  • Scene flow
  • Exploration track on robustness analysis

Keywords

Robustness, optical flow, stereo matching, scene flow, distribution shift.
